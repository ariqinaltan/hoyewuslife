Bow Wow Claims To Be Flying Private Jet, Gets Busted In Coach

Bow Wow has spent the majority of the day taking his share of social media-delivered L's. The former rapper, who is set to embark on a press tour for his upcoming appearance on Growing Up Hip Hop, shared this post detailing his lavish ride.

Unfortunately for Bow Wow, his portrayal of riches was soon exposed as a facade when the rapper was spotted flying coach. A keen-eyed Twitter user posted this picture of Bow Wow kicking it on a commercial flight, and put the Growing Up Hip Hop star on blast.

To add insult to injury, one internet sleuth reverse image searched the private jet picture, which apparently originated as a Fort Lauderdale Transport Company stock photo. It would appear that Bow Wow was caught in the middle of a false stunt, and social media users are never ones to let someone off easy.
