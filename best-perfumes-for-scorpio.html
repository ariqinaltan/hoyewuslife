Best Perfumes For Scorpios, According To An Astrologer

Shopping for perfume can seem like an impossible task. Even if you know which scent category you lean toward, there are hundreds of fragrances on the market with a plethora of note combinations that can make decision-making difficult. But since we already look to the stars for insights about our future, we figure they can also help guide us to our next signature scent.

In the spirit of Scorpio season, we tapped astrologer and author Lisa Stardust to see what perfumes this magnetic water sign leans toward.

"Scorpios like heavy and intense scents that can be smelled a mile away," she explains. "They like musky and dramatic fragrances that speak to their deep persona."

If you were born between Oct. 23 and Nov. 21, or are looking to buy a perfume for the zodiac's scorpion, keep reading to find five fragrances bound to be a match.

Best Warm and Spicy Perfume For Scorpios:

Yves Saint Laurent Black Opium Eau de Parfum

The exquisite cocktail of adrenaline-inducing black coffee with sweet vanilla and white flowers transforms your skin's natural scent into an intoxicatingly seductive aroma. You'll smell as passionate as a Scorpio's love feels and is sure to be just as addictive.

Best Floral Perfume For Scorpios:

Byredo Rose Noir

A dark take on the romantic rose, this fragrance is far from your grandma's bouquet. It blends Damascena rose with notes of musk, moss, and cardamom to give the traditionally sweet scent a mysteriously dark twist — perfect for any intense Scorpio.

Best Fruity Perfume For Scorpios:

Carolina Herrera Good Girl Eau de Parfum

As Stardust mentions, the scorpion of the galaxy leans toward dramatic fragrances, so it's no wonder that the fruity scent it's drawn to smells velvety and rich, too. While the rich base notes are made of cocoa, coffee bean, and tonka bean, the top of notes consist of jasmine and tuberose balance it out for the perfect fruity femme fatale scent.

Best Woody Perfume For Scorpios:

KKW Fragrance Kourtney — Ruby Diamond

Leave it to Kim Kardashian to create the perfect woody scent inspired by her energetic sister, Kourtney. Sandalwood, vanilla bean, and red jasmine make up the base of this captivating scent while ginger, roses, pink peppercorns, and pimento top it off to make it a scent to remember.

Best Fresh Perfume For Scorpios:

Maison Louis Marie No.04 Bois de Balincourt Perfume Oil

This clean fragrance is the perfect daytime scent for the water sign. The fresh, woody aroma smells lighter than heavier earthy scents while retaining the same magnetic allure. Roll the oil over your pressure points to enjoy whiffs of vetiver and cinnamon all day.
