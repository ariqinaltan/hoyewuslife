What is SSS-Class Suicide Hunter?

SSS-Class Suicide Hunter is a popular Korean web novel written by Shin Noah and illustrated by Kim Ji-hyuk. It tells the story of Kim Gong-ja, a man who has the unique ability to possess the bodies of those who killed him and inherit their skills and abilities. He uses this power to hunt down the strongest beings in the world, the SSS-Class Hunters, and become one of them.

The web novel has been adapted into a webtoon by the same name, which is serialized on KakaoPage and Tapas. The webtoon has attracted a huge fan base for its action-packed scenes, dark humor, and intriguing plot twists. The webtoon is currently in its third season, which started on May 26, 2023.

What happened in the previous chapter 101?

The latest chapter of the webtoon, chapter 101, was released on November 2, 2023. In this chapter, we saw Kim Gong-ja face off against the mysterious masked man who claimed to be his father. The masked man revealed his identity as Kim Seung-ho, the former leader of the SSS-Class Hunter team, the Seven Stars. He also explained his motive for killing Kim Gong-ja’s mother and framing him for it. He said that he wanted to awaken Kim Gong-ja’s true potential as a Suicide Hunter and that he was proud of his son’s achievements.

However, Kim Gong-ja was not moved by his father’s words and attacked him with his full power. He used his skills as a Sword Master, a Berserker, a Necromancer, and a Soul Eater to overwhelm his father. He also summoned the souls of his previous victims, including the SSS-Class Hunters he had killed, to aid him in his fight. He declared that he would kill his father and take his place as the strongest SSS-Class Hunter in the world.

The chapter ended with a cliffhanger, as Kim Seung-ho unleashed his own secret weapon: a black dragon that he had tamed and fused with. He said that he would show Kim Gong-ja the true meaning of despair and that he would make him regret his choice.
