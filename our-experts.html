Our Experts - Grunge

We certainly do try, but hey, we can't know EVERYTHING. These are some of the experts we collaborate with to make sure you're getting the best info out there.

Jess Ponce III

Celebrity Body Language Expert

Jess Ponce III is the author of "Everyday Celebrity: A Personal Branding Guide from a Hollywood Media Coach" and "A.W.E.S.O.M.E.: 7 Keys to Unlock the Speaker Within." As the titles of his books suggest, Ponce is an expert communicator who uses his skills to help train others in the arts of media and personal presentation. Some of Ponce's notable clients include Lucasfilm, ABC, Disney Channel, E! Entertainment, FX, TV Guide Network, and MTV. Of course, Ponce's work and background also grant him special insight into nonverbal communication.

Static Media has consulted with Jess Ponce III for his expert opinions on celebrity body language.

Mark Bowden

Celebrity Body Language Expert

Mark Bowden — bestselling author of four books, speaker, and body language expert — is the founder and president of TRUTHPLANE, a communication training company. Having appeared frequently on CNN and CBS as a political and celebrity body language expert, he has also consulted with Toyota, American Express, NATO, and many others on effective communication techniques for leadership and success. He developed the GesturePlane system of body language to help people succeed in communication. He was once voted the top body language professional in the world by Global Gurus, and he is the president of the National Communication Coach Association of Canada.

Static Media has consulted with Mark Bowden for his expert opinions on celebrity body language.

Neama Rahmani

President, West Coast Trial Lawyers

The president and co-founder of West Coast Trial Lawyers, Neama Rahmani is a personal injury lawyer working in California. One of the youngest graduates of Harvard Law School, Rahmani was hired by O'Melveny & Myers and represented large corporations, ranging from Disney to Marriott. From there, he moved on to work for the US Attorney's office, prosecuting drug and human trafficking cases, even once successfully prosecuting a murderer and druglord who had appeared on "America's Most Wanted." Later on, he was appointed Director of Enforcement of the Los Angeles City Ethics Commission. Static Media has consulted with Neama Rahmani for his expert opinion on matters of celebrity legal issues.
