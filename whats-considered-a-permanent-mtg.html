Whats considered a permanent MTG?

A permanent in Magic: The Gathering (MTG) refers to a card or token that is on the battlefield. Once a permanent enters the battlefield, it remains there until it is destroyed, exiled, sacrificed, or otherwise removed according to the game rules. Permanents can be artifacts, creatures, enchantments, lands, or planeswalker cards.

Does a sorcery count as a permanent?

What’s a noncreature permanent?

A noncreature permanent is any permanent card that is not a creature. Examples of noncreature permanents include artifacts, enchantments, lands, and planeswalker cards.

Do cards in graveyards count as permanents?

Is an artifact a permanent?
