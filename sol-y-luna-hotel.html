Sol y Luna Hotel Review, Sacred Valley, Peru

The more formal Killa Wasi restaurant serves fine Peruvian dining, including ceviches, steaks, guinea pig and experimental dishes made using mud; the cocktails are excellent. Guests breakfast lavishly on fruit, cereals, fresh pastries and good coffee at the larger Wayra restaurant, which also does main dishes as well as burgers, pastas and sandwiches for lunch and dinner.
