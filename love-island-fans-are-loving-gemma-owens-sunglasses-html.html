Love Island fans are loving Gemma Owens sunglasses

Gemma Owen, daughter of football legend Michael Owen, has captured the hearts of Love Island viewers this season. Initially met with uncertainty, Gemma has won praise for her mature demeanor and unwavering loyalty throughout the show.

A Love Story Unfolds

At just 19, Gemma is the youngest contestant this season, and she’s found love with Luca Bish. Their connection has weathered the storm of Case Amor, making them one of the villa’s strongest couples. They’re now contenders for the coveted £50k cash prize.

The Sunglasses Sensation

Gemma’s fashion sense, particularly her dazzling array of sunglasses, has garnered her a dedicated fan following. Viewers are eager to emulate her style, prompting the question: Where can one find shades like Gemma’s?

Discover Gemma’s Style

Love Island has teamed up with Quay, a sunglasses emporium with a global reach, boasting 2,000 retailers across 25 countries. To explore the official Love Island collection, visit Quay Australia online. The best part? These islander-approved shades are affordably priced, ranging from £39 to £55, and may even see further reductions.

More Than Just Shades

Quay’s offerings extend beyond sunglasses. The website features a diverse range, including spectacles, blue-light lenses, and accessories. Whether you’re seeking stylish eyewear or practical options, Quay has you covered.

Gemma’s Luxe Eyewear

For those with an inclination towards luxury, Gemma Owen’s penchant for Dita sunglasses stands out. She often sports the ‘Epiluxury 4’, a high-end choice retailing at £1.8k. It’s a statement piece for those with a taste for opulence.

A Style for Every Budget

While Gemma’s Dita sunglasses exude sophistication, many fans lean towards the Quay brand for its accessibility and reasonable pricing. It begs the question: Is a hefty price tag a prerequisite for islander-worthy style?

Gemma Owen’s sunglasses have become a sensation, sparking a fashion frenzy among Love Island enthusiasts. Whether you opt for the affordable allure of Quay or indulge in the luxury of Dita, one thing is certain – Gemma’s style shines as bright as the sun-drenched villa itself.
