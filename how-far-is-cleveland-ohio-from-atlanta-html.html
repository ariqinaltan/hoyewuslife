Just How Far is Cleveland From Atlanta By Plane?

The answer, my friend, is not as far as you might think! In the grand scheme of the ol' globe, Atlanta and Cleveland are practically neighbors. We're talking a hop, skip, and a short jump (because let's face it, airplanes don't hop).

Here's the nitty gritty:

  • The Distance: The distance between Atlanta, Georgia and Cleveland, Ohio is roughly 554 miles (or 893 kilometers for our international friends).
  • The Flight Time: Buckle up for some serious relaxation time (or a chance to catch up on your favorite podcast). The average flight time between these two cities is a cool 1 hour and 42 minutes. That's less time than it takes to watch some movies!

FAQs for the Aspiring Cleveland Flyer

Alright, alright, we know you have questions. Here are the quick answers to some of the most frequent ones:

  • How to find a flight from Atlanta to Cleveland? There are a number of online travel agencies and airline websites where you can search for flights.
  • How long does it take to get to the airport in Atlanta? Depending on where you're staying, allow yourself enough time to get to Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport (ATL) with traffic in mind.
  • What's the weather like in Cleveland? Cleveland has four distinct seasons, so pack accordingly for the time of year you're visiting.
  • What should I pack for my trip to Cleveland? Cleveland is a casual city, so pack comfortable clothes and shoes.
  • What are some must-see attractions in Cleveland? The Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, the Cleveland Museum of Art, and the West Side Market are all great places to start.
