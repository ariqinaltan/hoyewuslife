Martin Scorsese Getting Daniel Day-Lewis To Unretire?

Overall, Killer of the Flower Moon took home Best Film, Best Director, Outstanding Achievement in Cinematography, and Lily Gladstone won Best Actress for the film. 

After receiving a Golden Globe award just a few days before the National Board of Review Awards ceremony, Lily Gladstone was somewhat at a loss for words when she first took the mic after Martin Scorsese was surprised by Daniel Day-Lewis’s appearance.

“This isn’t real. This isn’t real”, she repeated. “Daniel Day-Lewis, did that just happen”?
