How do I change my age restriction on Epic?

To change your age restriction on Epic, you can follow these steps:

  • Sign into the Epic Account Portal or go into the Fortnite Parental Controls menu
  • Select Parental Controls
  • Enter your six-digit PIN at the prompt
  • Choose the desired on/off state for each setting

    • Why is my epic account age restricted?

    If an account’s birthdate is under the age of 13 or their country’s age of digital consent, whichever is higher, their account becomes a Cabined Account. Parental consent is required for these accounts to access certain features in Epic Games’ products.

    How does Epic Games know your age?

    Epic Games knows a player’s age if they indicate they are under 13 or their country’s age of digital consent. In this case, they will be asked to provide a parent or guardian’s email address for consent to access certain features.

    How do you verify you are an adult on Epic Games?

    Epic Games verifies if a player is an adult by using Social Security Number verification in the US. Other information such as first name, last name, date of birth, address, zip code, and the last 4 digits of their social security number are checked against a government database to confirm their age.

    How do I get parental permission on Epic Games?

    To set up Parental Controls for the Epic Games Store, you can follow these steps:

  • Go to your account settings via the Epic Games Launcher or the Epic Games Store website
  • Sign in to your account
  • Ensure you’re on the Parental Controls tab on the left side of the screen
  • You should see the “Set Parental Controls PIN” window

    • How do I turn off verification on Epic Games?

    To turn off verification on Epic Games, you can follow these steps:

  • Go to the ACCOUNT page
  • Click the PASSWORD & SECURITY tab
  • Under the TWO-FACTOR AUTHENTICATION header, click the “On/Off” toggle button related to the 2FA method you want to disable and switch it off

    • What is the minimum age for Epic Games Fortnite?

    The minimum age to be eligible to compete in a Fortnite competitive event is 13 years old or the age required by your country of residence, whichever is greater.

    What age rating is Fortnite epic?

    Fortnite has an age recommendation of 13 and up. It is rated “T” for teens by the Entertainment Software Ratings Board due to its action violence and open chat.

    How do I change parental email on Epic Games?

    To change your parental email on Epic Games, you can follow these steps:

  • Log in to your Epic Games account
  • Go to the Account portal
  • In the ACCOUNT INFO section, click the Edit button next to your email address
  • In the pop-up window, enter your new email address and click CONFIRM

    • How do I remove parental control restrictions?

    To remove parental control restrictions for the Google Play Store, you can follow these steps:

  • Open the Google Play Store app
  • Tap your profile image in the upper-right corner
  • Tap Settings
  • Tap Family
  • Tap Parental Controls
  • Tap a category (e.g., “Apps & Games,” “Movies,” TV,” “Books”)
  • Enter your PIN
  • Tap Allow All and tap Save

    • How can I delete parental control?

    To delete parental control, you can follow these steps:

  • Open the Family Link app on your Android device
  • Tap on the child’s account in question
  • Tap “Manage settings”
  • Tap “Controls on Google Play”
  • Edit your parental controls, even if your child is younger than 13

    • How do I delete my Epic parent account?

    To delete your Epic parent account for Android/Google Play Subscription, you can follow these steps:

  • Click on ‘Parent Dashboard’ and enter your password when prompted
  • Click on Settings
  • Click ‘Manage’ (next to Subscription Status)
  • Click on ‘Cancel Subscription’

    • Do you have to be 18 to use uefn?

    To use UEFN, you must be at least 18 years old. However, if you are below 18, you can ask your parents to publish your game for you.

    Why is my Fortnite chat age restricted?

    Your Fortnite chat is age-restricted because there are certain settings in place depending on your age. For children under the age of 10, the max setting allowed for both Voice chat and Text chat is “Friends Only”.

    Is Fortnite OK for a 7-year-old?

    Common Sense Media recommends Fortnite for teens 13 and up due to its open chat and action violence. However, each child is different, so parental discretion is advised.

    Is Fortnite age 12?

    Fortnite is rated 12+ by PEGI due to frequent scenes of mild violence.

    Is Fortnite Rated R?

    Fortnite is rated T for Teen by the Entertainment Software Ratings Board with a Content Descriptor for Violence and Interactive Elements including Users Interact and In-Game Purchases.

    Can you set an age range on Fortnite?

    Yes, you can set age restrictions or parental controls for Fortnite through the Epic Account Portal or using the Fortnite Parental Controls menu.

    How do I change privacy settings on Epic Games?

    To change privacy settings on Epic Games, you can follow these steps:

  • Navigate to the “My Achievements” page on Epic Games
  • Click the dotted button next to Privacy Level
  • Select Manage Privacy

    • How do I verify my Epic Games?

    To verify your Epic Games, you can follow these steps:

  • Open the Epic Games Launcher
  • Find the game in your Epic Games Library
  • Click the three dots on the right side of the game line
  • Select Manage
  • Click the Verify button
  • Wait for validation to complete

    • Why is Epic Games asking for verification?

    Epic Games may ask for verification if a player indicates they are a child and privacy laws require parental consent for access to certain features that collect personal information.
