Lola Albright Net Worth

Lola Albright was born on July 20, 1925 in Akron, OH. She is best known for her role as Edie Hart in the television series Peter Gunn, and for her starring role in the 1962 film Kid Galahad. She was a popular actress in the 1950s and 1960s, and her career spanned over five decades.

About

She played Edie Hart, the title character's love interest in Peter Gunn, and starred in the 1962 film Kid Galahad.

Before Fame

She had an unremarkable job as a switchboard operator for a local radio station, which led to several minor roles and a modeling job.
