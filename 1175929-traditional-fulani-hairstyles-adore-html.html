Traditional Fulani hairstyles you will adore

Here we have another great topic dedicated to traditional Fulani hairstyles. The culture of this tribe is very rich, and they know how to create an incredibly beautiful look. Read on to know the secrets of great hairstyle tradition.

Fulani tribe is spread over West Africa, and the biggest community is located in Nigeria. The tribe people rely on their great traditions, that is why their appearance is full of traditional trends even today.

Traditional Fulani hairstyles

Usually, women of the tribe tend to grow long hair, it is considered a symbol of femininity and beauty. Braided hair is also very popular. There is a tradition to split the hair into five parts and then make them into a shape of braids. Usually one can see family jewelry added into braids. They can add precious stones, valuable coins or some other decorations to the hair. As for Fulani wedding hairstyles, they usually show the most beautiful elements of a traditional look.

So, let's consider traditional Fulani hairstyle in more details:

  • Fulani braid hairstyles

Braids are very popular among the women of the tribe. Most often they do cornrows braids and didi braids. In most cases the braids are woven in elaborate ornament, the ends hang from the sides or bundled. They decorate the braids with jewelry or red beads.

The braids are usually styled with thin-to-medium patterns. Actually, today Fulani braids hairstyles are popular all over the world. Modern designers actively use this hairstyle and combine it with other styles. We can see top buns, different colors of braids, various patterns and numerous beads.

  • Decorations in the hairstyles

Fulani people are famous for adding various decorations to the hair. Among most popular decorations we can see veils. The veil is underlining one part of the head and hide all the rest. The thread is another popular decoration, they preferably are made of gold.

The next variation of the decoration is jewelry or beads. The bigger - the better, this is the rule for this type of the decoration.

  • Braid ornaments

The ornaments can be very difficult and sophisticated. Patterns, circles, intertwinings - all these features can be seen in Fulani hairstyles.

The beauty of traditional Fulani hairstyles is recognized all over the world. The braids have become popular among many celebrities not only in Africa. That is why we can say that Fulani tradition of hairstyles made an influence on world's fashion.

Fulani people continue to follow their traditions of beauty and support their culture even these days.
