XXXTentacion &amp; Lil Pump Connect On &quot;Arms Around You&quot; Featuring Swae Lee &amp; Maluma

XXXTentacion has been all over the news this week after Pitchfork released some exclusive audio of the late XXXTentacion talking about beating his girlfriend. In fact, X can be heard detailing how he made his ex-girlfriend “scared for her life,” which isn’t a good look by any means. But despite all the negative publicity, X’s fans are still defending the Florida rapper’s legacy, and now today there treated to a new song with Lil Pump called “Arms Around You,” featuring Swae Lee & Maluma. The song was first teased by Skrilez on Instagram last night, where he tagged all the artists in the clip, and revealing that the song was dropping today, October 25th, and alas here it is.

Coincidentally, X handles the chorus and raps about wrapping his arms around his girl, “to make sure no one could harm you,” which is rather ironic considering he’s accused of beating his ex-girlfriend. Produced by Skrillex, Jon Fx & Mally Mall, take a listen to the new collab and let us know what you think. Record available on iTunes.
