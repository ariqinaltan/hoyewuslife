New DLC Character Pack Confirmed For AEW Fight Forever Video Game

AEW truly changed the face of pro wrestling thanks to its introduction as an alternative to WWE. Less than a year into its inception, the company announced its first-ever game called Fight Forever. The game has been released for almost three months now and it appears a new DLC character pack has been confirmed for the game.

The game was released on June 29th and fans and critics alike have praised it for its solid and addictive gameplay but criticized the game for its lack of quality-of-life features as well as very limited options for customization.

The game will be receiving regular updates and new game modes every once in a while. AEW Games’ official Twitter account took to the social media site and announced the new “HOOKHausen: Very Handsome, Very Evil” DLC pack will be dropping on September 28 for Xbox, PlayStation, and PC.

Check out these sneak peek screenshots of the @730hook and@DanhausenAD models in #AEWFightForever!

The HOOKHausen: Very Handsome, Very Evil Pack is coming to AEW: Fight Forever on September 28th for Xbox, PlayStation and PC. Nintendo Switch to follow.

That said, the video game has already been discounted to $10 in select stores recently. We will have to see whether the latest DLC will bring back interest in the video game son.
