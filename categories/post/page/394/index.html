'Carrie' Remake Actress Was 28

published on August 11, 2024
Samantha Weinstein, a Canadian actress since childhood who played high school student Heather in the 2013 remake of Carrie, has died. She was 28. Weinstein died May 14 at Princess Margaret Hospital in Toronto after a battle with ovarian cancer. James Crammond, her agent at Noble Caplan Abrams in Toronto, said Weinstein worked up until May 4, doing an animation voice session. “A trouper to the end. We all miss her terribly,” Crammond told The Hollywood Reporter.
