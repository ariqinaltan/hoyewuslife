Actress Adaku of Jenifa's Diary clocks 42, shares stunning photos

A popular comic actress in the Nigerian movie industry recently shared some stunning photos in celebration of herself as she celebrates a new age.

The actress identified as Omotunde Adebowale, who is very much known for her role as Adaku in popular comic series Jenifa’s Diary, clocked the age of 42 and she could not hide her excitement as she shared the news with members of the online community.

Posting a series of absolutely fabulous photos on Instagram, the actress and Wazobia FM radio presenter expressed gratefulness to the lord and revealed that her heart is filled with nothing but joy.

"I am grateful lord. Chapter 42 loaded. I have nothing but joy in my heart.Happy birthday to me." she said.
