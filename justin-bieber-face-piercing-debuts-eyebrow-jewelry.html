Justin Bieber Face Piercing: Debuts Eyebrow Jewelry

Brace yourselves, you guys, because Justin Bieber just debuted a brand new face piercing, and the internet is going wild over it. Yep, that’s right. The singer has pierced his right eyebrow, and boy, does it look swoon-worthy!

The “Love Yourself” crooner showed off the new look via Instagram on Tuesday, July 23. He posted a picture of himself lounging outside and catching some rays, and if you look closely, you’ll notice the small piece of jewelry above his right eye.

Justin also flaunted a diamond grill on his lower teeth. The Biebs and his wife, Hailey Baldwin, were spotted hitting up Ancient Adornments in West Hollywood on Sunday for his new piercing. And according to Page Six, the model also went under the needle! She reportedly got two new piercings in her upper ear.

And it turns out, this isn’t the 25-year-old’s first piercing. Back in 2016, Justin was spotted with a nose piercing for a few weeks. He’s also been rocking earrings for a quite some time now. Oh, and did we mention that his whole body is practically covered with tattoos?

Any time one of our favorite celebrities changes up their appearance, it’s always a big deal. So naturally, his fans quickly went into a frenzy over the new look.

“Justin‘s eyebrow piercing looks HOT,” one fan wrote on Twitter, while another added, “Justin + piercing = perfection.”
