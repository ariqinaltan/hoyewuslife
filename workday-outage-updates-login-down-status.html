Workday outage updates Workers reporting issues with website login and complain app is down

Workday spokesperson confirms outage

In a statement to The U.S. Sun, a Workday spokesperson said: "Due to a brief power outage at one of our data centers, some customers are currently experiencing a service interruption.

"Power has since been restored, and we are in the process of getting impacted customers back online as quickly as possible.

"We are providing regular updates to impacted customers."

Reports reach over 800

Issue reports on Downdetector have continued to rise Thursday morning.

As of 10 am, over 820 users reported problems with Workday.

Users flood X

Workday users have flooded X, formerly known as Twitter, to address problems with the online service.

The beginning of Workday

Workday was founded by Dave Duffield and Aneel Bhusri in 2005.

The founders were previously executives at PeopleSoft.

In addition to being a co-founder, Bhusri is also listed as the company's Co-CEO, and Chair.

Duffield is listed as Workday's CEO Emeritus.

More about Workday

Workday is a cloud-based software vendor.

The company is specialized in human capital management and financial management applications

Workday is headquartered in Pleasanton, California.
