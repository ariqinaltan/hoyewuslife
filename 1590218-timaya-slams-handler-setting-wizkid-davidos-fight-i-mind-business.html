Timaya Slams Handler for Setting Him Up Over Wizkid and Davidos Fight: I Mind My Business

  • A comment on Nigerian singer Timaya's Twitter account (now known as X) following the war in the music industry brought him negative attention
  • Recall that Aforobeats stars Wizkid and Davido shook the industry with their online feud, as they threw insults at each other
  • Timaya has come out to publicly criticise a member of his team who made the post about the fight on his personal X account
  • Isreal Chukwuebuka, a digital communication expert, in a chat with Legit.ng pointed at who made the incriminating post

Nigerian singer Inetimi Timaya Odon, also known as Timaya, has publicly called out one of his employees, Bolaji Fadipe, for involving him in Wizkid and Davido's fight. 

Timaya was chastised online for referring to Davido and Wizkid's confrontation as "industry wars."

This was as a result of a post Bolaji Fadipe reportedly made via the musician's X account on April 29.

Hours later, the dancehall artist responded and clarified that the tweet had been made by one of his team members, who had access.

Timaya doesn't get involved in drama - Digital communication expert
