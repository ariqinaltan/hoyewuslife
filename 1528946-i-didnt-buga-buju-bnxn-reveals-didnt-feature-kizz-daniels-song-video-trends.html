I Didnt Like Buga: Buju BNXN Reveals Why He Didnt Feature on Kizz Daniels Song, Video

  • Nigerian singer Buju BNXN has sparked major conversation online after he made a shocking revelation about his colleague Kizz Daniel and his 2022 hit track Buga
  • Buju, during an interview in Ghana, said he didn't like the song, and it wasn't his type of sound which was why he didn't feature on it
  • The pair later got to work together on another track, Gwagwalada

Reigning Headies Award Next Rated Artist of the Year, Buju BNXN has sparked a controversial conversation online with a comment he made during his recent visit to Ghana.

During an interview in Ghana with Pluzz FM, Buju revealed that he didn't feature on Kizz Daniel's 2022 monster hit track, Buga, because he felt it wasn't his type of sound.

BNXN, during the interview, also made several other revelations. He said Kizz Daniel played the track Buga before it was released, but he turned down the opportunity to feature in it.
