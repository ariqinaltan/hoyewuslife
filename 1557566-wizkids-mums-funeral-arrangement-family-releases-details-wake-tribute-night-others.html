Wizkids Mums Funeral Arrangement: Family Releases Details on Wake, Tribute Night, Others

  • Wizkid's family has released the funeral arrangement for the singer's late mother, Mrs Jane Dolapo Balogun
  • According to details provided on a viral flyer, the wake and tribute night will take place on Thursday, October 12, followed by a funeral service on Friday, October 13
  • The recent update has stirred reactions from many of Wizkid's fans amid his silence on social media

Months after music star Wizkid's mother, Mrs Jane Dolapo Balogun, passed on, the singer's family has released details about her funeral arrangement.

A flyer sighted by Legit.ng on social media revealed the deceased's wake and tribute night will take place on Thursday, October 12, followed by a funeral service on Friday, October 13, at RCCG City of David, Victoria Island, Lagos.
