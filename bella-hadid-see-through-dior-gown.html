Bella Hadid Wears See-Through Dior Dress at Couture Week

Like a true boss, Bella Hadid showed up at Dior's Masquerade Ball last night with no mask and also no bra. The supermodel attended the Couture Week party wearing a sheer gown from Dior's Spring 2017 collection. The end result was Disney princess meets the #FreetheNipple movement, which is apparently a look that I can very much get behind.

Getty Images

Hadid showed up to the party alongside Kendall Jenner, who also wore a sheer dress and decided to wear sunglasses instead of a mask (important life lesson: when you're a model you don't wear masks or bras, okay?).

After a paparazzi photo of Hadid's ex, The Weeknd, kissing Selena Gomez surfaced last week, the model was reportedly "upset" but it looks like she's doing just fine. Inside the party, she hung out with friends Jenner, A$AP Rocky and ASAP Ferg.
