Pear Farmers Thank Rick Ross For The Shoutout

If you've been anywhere on the internet in the past month, you may have come across a now legendary vine of Rick Ross giving a shout out to "all the pear". The rapper has recently lost a ton of weight on his self-described "Ross-fit" diet, and was simply describing the benefits of his lifestyle change in the Tim Westwood interview. 

The quote has become quite a sensation, to the point that Ross' Instagram comment section has been berated by pear emojis. 

All jokes aside, pear farmers are actually quite grateful for Rozay's endorsement of the fruit, with USA Pears’ spokeswoman (yes, that's a real title) Kathy Stephenson making a statement today.

“On behalf of our 1,600 pear growers from the Northwest region, we are pleased that Rick has mentioned the health benefits of pears... Overall, we could not be more pleased with our message being delivered to a new, young and hip demographic.”

Hood Billionaire is out November 24th.
