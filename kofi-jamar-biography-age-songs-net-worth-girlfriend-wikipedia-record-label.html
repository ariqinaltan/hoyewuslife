Biography

Derrick Osei Kuffour Prempeh (born 7 April 1995) is known professionally by his stage name, Kofi Jamar.

He is a well-known and upcoming Ghanaian rapper, singer, and songwriter.

The Ghanaian rapper joined the Ghanaian entertainment industry in 2019 and has achieved widespread fame for his song, Roll-On.

Early Life

Kofi Jamar was born on the 7th day of April in 1995. The popular entertainer was born and raised in Bantama located in Kumasi, Ghana.

His passion for music started from a young age and he was supported fully by his parents. He was raised in a music-loving household and never lacked the basic necessities of life.

Education

Kofi Jamar started and completed his probably and secondary school education in Kumasi, Ghana.

Career

Kofi Jamar started his music career in 2018. Jamar was introduced to the Ghanaian music industry by remixing the cover of Johnny, a song released by the well-known Nigerian female artiste, Yemi Alade.

He remixed this cover with the Ghanaian rapper, Obey. This remix was loved by fans and made him popular across Ghana. The remix became an instant hit in Ghana and as a result, he gained a large audience and fans in Ghana. He was called the most promising artist in the Ashanti region of Ghana but unfortunately for the lack of financial support and proper advertisement, he could not meet the high expectations of the fans.

In 2018, he was signed by a Ghanaian-based Virginian record label called, Gardone Records which took interest in his talents. He is currently working on releasing several songs. He is yet to release his first album but he is currently working on it and his fans are in expectation and this time he is prepared for it.

At the young age of eighteen, he entered into a freestyle rap competition organized by a top Ghanaian radio station based in Kumasi. As a result of this unique talent, he won the competition and was crowned the best rapper in the Kumasi area of Ghana. Well-known names in the Ghanaian music and rap industry won this same award including Strongman (rapper) and Ypee.
