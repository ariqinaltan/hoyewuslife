Rihanna Announces Release Date for Fenty x Puma Collection

Update, 8/30: The only thing that could be better than a collection designed by a style icon, is being able to shop it with her.

To celebrate her Fenty x Puma debut, Rihanna will be making special appearances in New York City when her collection launches on Tuesday, Sept. 6. She'll be stopping by Bergdorf Goodman for a private shopping event with VIP customers, and then at her Fenty x Puma pop-up shop at 125 West 33rd Street. The store will be open to the public, but it does have a limited capacity, so only a select group of fans will be able to shop with RiRi herself.

Even if you don't get the chance to meet the singer-turned-designer, you can still shop her debut collection at Bergdorf's and her Fenty x Puma pop-ups (the second location is at 8380 Melrose Avenue in L.A.) staring next Tuesday.

Original post, 8/22: Rihanna's debut Fenty x Puma collection is dropping on September 6, the singer just revealed on her Twitter and Instagram this morning. Riri coupled the announcement with a fitting photo of herself wrapped up in a hoodie from the brand.

The collection, which consists of women's and menswear, premiered on the runway in February at NYFW, with the likes of Gigi and Bella Hadid in the model lineup. Looks evoked a combination of goth-chic and streetwear—reminiscent of Rihanna's own style—with athletic silhouettes, sheer motifs and lace-up accents in a black and white color scheme.
