Various career finders find employment in the extended network of Speedway’s fueling stations. The store locations of Speedway are usually located near busy junctions or on most traveled highways. The Job Positions Available at Speedway

Speedway offers multiple employment positions, including General Store Manager, Assistant Store Manager, Maintenance Technician, and Food Steward. It also provides Shift Leader, Gas Station Attendant, Cashier, Sales Associate, and Customer Service Representative positions. Those looking for work at a gas station are likely to find jobs in the following areas:

Customer Service Representatives

  • Customer Service Representatives are responsible for promoting in-house reward initiatives and stocking the products.
  • They are also responsible for ringing up purchases, assisting and greeting customers.
  • Speedway usually initiates their career earning a minimum wage and may rise making approximately $08.00 to $09.00 per hour.

Food Service Specialists

  • Food Service Specialists are responsible for ensuring proper food presentation, replenishing supplies and materials.
  • They are also responsible for serving and preparation of drinks and food.
  • Speedway usually offers a minimum wage to its Food Service Specialists.

Management

  • Job titles for management-level employees include manager, co-manager, and shift leader.
  • Shift Leaders are responsible for supervising general duties and preparing store closing and opening.
  • Shift Leaders earn approximately $09.00 to $10.00 per hour at Speedway.
  • Managers and co-managers at Speedway earn approximately $30k to $50k per annum.

The Online Job Application Process of Speedway

Speedway’s online job application process is as follows:

  • Look out Speedway’s official website and go to its employment webpage.
  • Tap on the “Apply” tab to choose the job role you want to apply for at Speedway.
  • Choose your preferred city or state from the drop-down list that appeared to enter the ZIP code in the blank field and locate your location.
  • Tap on the desired location and review your preferred job title requirements, position responsibilities, and requirements.
  • Tap on the “Apply Now” can agree to the pre-application disclosures that appeared here.
  • Upload your resume only if you want to. It is an optional choice for the candidate.
  • Enter your personal information in the blank fields and complete the following section to submit your job application to Speedway.

Checking Job Application Status for Speedway

Once applicants submit hiring forms, the gas station chain evaluates the information and calls suitable individuals to organize job interviews. In most situations, candidates get notifications from Speedway to attend interviews within a week.

Individuals can choose whether to follow up on job applications before receiving a call or e-mail from management.
