The Reported Reason Behind G-Eazy And Halsey's Split: G-Eazy &quot;All Over Other Girls&quot;

Yesterday, it was reported that musicians Halsey and G-Eazy had once again ended their relationship after having reconciled a few months ago. 

The couple went through a public and storied breakup earlier this year. G-Eazy was allegedly unfaithful to Halsey, becoming involved romantically with other women. Halsey shared news of the breakup to her Instagram story in July writing “G-Eazy and I are taking some time apart. I’m eager to continue the upcoming passage of time dedicating myself to my art and my career and the duration of my tour. I wish him the best. " Not long after the post, the couple were spotted together once again. They seemed to confirm rumours of a reconciliation with a joint appearance at the American Music Awards a few weeks ago. 

Now, the two have allegedly split again, and have notably unfollowed each other on social media. A source told US Weekly that “They just can’t seem to get past their previous issues." The source went on to suggest that the Bay area rapper's flirtatious behaviour may have prompted the couples split adding that he  “was all over other girls publicly at his Halloween party this past weekend.”
